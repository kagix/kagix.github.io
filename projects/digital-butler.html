Digital Butler

Digital Butler helps people manage their own computer files. In a world where important documents mix with disposable junk from the internet, the butler is helping you to organize your digital valuables. It tracks any type of documents, including text, audio and video. You know who is using sensitive documents before people mishandle them.

The Digital Butler works on Windows, Mac and Linux. It comes in two flavors: personal and enterprise.
