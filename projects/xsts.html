XSTS

XSTS is about improving the user ride experience in public transportation. Many city councils have little to no budget for developping better transit service. What could have been considered true 50 years ago can and should be heavily challenged in the age of digital information. This is not a project but a framework made of many layers and bricks, generating information that can make rider's life better.

XSTS is written in Java and you can download the code for free from the official repository.
